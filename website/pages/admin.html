Cost inputs management

COGS per SKU, packaging cost default, shipping cost rules. Only admins can change these.

Current settings (demo)

  • COGS per SKU: From metafield / CSV import; override per variant in app.
  • Packaging cost default: £0.45 per order (configurable).
  • Shipping cost rules: Flat by zone or % of order; configurable in Settings.

Fee model toggles

Shopify fee %, payment fee %, FX fee %. Applied at order level then allocated by revenue share.

  • Shopify fee %: 2.0% (demo)
  • Payment fee %: 2.9% + £0.30 (demo)
  • FX fee %: 2.0% when multi-currency (demo)

Margin thresholds

Warn below X%; critical below 0%. Drives risk flags and decision prompts.

  • Warn below: 10% margin (configurable)
  • Critical below: 0% (negative margin)

Allocation options (Phase 2)

Ads allocation method: revenue share, equal split, or manual. Demo dropdown below—live product would persist selection.
