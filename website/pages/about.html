About EORE

System-led, scalable approach—not founder-led hero story. Honest about stage.

Why this exists

Merchants need margin truth. Too many scale on revenue while hidden costs destroy profit per SKU. EORE exists to give you SKU-level net margin, standardised fee models, and a decision workflow with review cadence—so you scale what actually makes money.

Built for real operators

We’re built for people who run or advise Shopify stores. System-led, not founder-led. The product delivers repeatable margin checks and decision logs so outcomes don’t depend on a single person. That improves audit readiness and reduces wrong or delayed decisions.

Early-stage MVP: honest status

We don’t claim customers we don’t have. Current status: scaffold exists; integration and engine logic in progress; private beta next. We’re aiming for a beta with 5–10 merchants to validate the workflow and then iterate. No hype—engine-first MVP, then expand.

Why independent / neutral

EORE is independent of ad platforms, payment processors, or specific agencies. We focus on margin modelling and decision workflow so you can align the system with your own priorities—no vendor lock-in to a particular ecosystem.
