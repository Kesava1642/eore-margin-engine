Product / Services

EORE is delivered as product components—not consulting. Below are the modules and what you get.

Decision Engine

Input → logic → output. You feed in the situation (incident type, severity, constraints). The engine applies your rules and playbooks and returns a recommended next action, escalation path, or checklist. Configurable thresholds and priorities so the logic matches your site.

Incident Playbooks (first 90 minutes)

Structured steps for the critical first 90 minutes: triage, communication, escalation, and recovery. Reduces variation across shifts and ensures nothing is missed. Templates are editable by admins so they stay aligned with your operations.

Decision Logs

Every recommendation and every action is recorded—timestamp, store/site, actor, inputs, outputs, status. Full audit trail for compliance and post-incident review. Filter by type, severity, date, and owner.

Admin Control

Roles, permissions, and approvals. Only admins change settings, run syncs, and apply write-backs. Non-admin users can view dashboards and insights. Escalation contacts and approval thresholds are configurable per site.

What you get

  • Decision Engine with configurable rules and playbooks
  • Incident playbooks for the first 90 minutes
  • Decision log and audit trail
  • Admin dashboard: metrics, roles, escalation, approval thresholds
  • Decision tracking: planned action, applied?, expected impact, review date, outcome status

Viability

Who pays: Warehouse site operator / 3PL site GM.

Who uses: Ops manager + supervisors.

Value: Reduced downtime, fewer wrong decisions, audit readiness.
