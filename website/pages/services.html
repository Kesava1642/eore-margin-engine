Product / Services

EORE delivers profit intelligence for Shopify stores. Below are the modules and what you get. We state MVP scope (Phase 1) and next phases honestly.

Core Margin Engine

True net margin per SKU. Connect your store; we pull orders and line items, map revenue and discounts, apply your fee and cost assumptions, and compute net profit and margin % at SKU level. So you see which products are safe to scale, which need repricing, and which are loss-makers.

Cost & Fee Modelling

Shopify fees, gateway/payment fees, shipping, discounts, refunds, FX. Configurable at store level so your margin view reflects reality. Packaging and COGS per SKU (or defaults) so you’re not guessing.

Allocation Logic

Ad spend + refunds allocation per SKU — Phase 2. We’ll support allocation methods (e.g. revenue share, equal split, or manual rules) so you can attribute ad cost and refund impact to SKUs and see true net margin after marketing and returns.

Margin Risk Flags

Negative margin, low-margin threshold, leakage hotspots. The system flags risk and generates decision prompts (reprice, pause ads, change discount, update COGS). Standardised fee models and repeatable margin checks—works across multiple stores on tiered plans.

Exportable Reporting

CSV export so you can audit, share with accountants, or feed into other tools. Decision workflow with review cadence; every recommendation and action is logged.

MVP scope (Phase 1) vs Next (Phase 2/3)

Phase 1 (MVP): Connect Shopify, pull orders, map revenue/discounts, apply fee models and cost inputs, compute net profit and margin %, flag negative/low margin, decision log and tracking. Export CSV.

Phase 2/3: Ad spend allocation per SKU, refund allocation options, multi-currency/FX refinement, and more allocation methods. We’re honest about stage: engine-first MVP, then expand.
