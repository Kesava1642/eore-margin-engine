EORE — Shopify Margin Engine

Profit clarity engine. SKU-level net margin so you scale what actually makes money. Repeatable margin checks and a decision workflow with review cadence—works across multiple stores.

The problem

Shopify merchants scale on revenue while hidden costs—fees, shipping, discounts, refunds, ad spend, packaging, FX—silently destroy profit per SKU. Revenue-only thinking leads to scaling loss-makers, cashflow shocks, and wrong pricing. You need margin truth, not just top-line numbers.

Who it is for

EORE is built for people who run or advise Shopify stores:

  • Shopify dropshippers — solo operators who need to know which products are actually profitable
  • Small DTC brand owners — margin clarity before scaling spend
  • Growth marketers / performance ads managers — reallocate ad spend away from loss SKUs
  • Ecommerce agencies — managing multiple stores with consistent fee and cost assumptions

Decisions we improve

  • Which products are safe to scale? — True net margin per SKU, not just revenue
  • Which need repricing? — Flag low-margin and negative-margin SKUs
  • Which should be paused? — Stop-loss SKUs before they burn cash
  • Where is margin leakage? — Fees, shipping, discounts, refunds surfaced and allocated
  • How to reallocate ad spend? — Phase-2: allocation logic so you don’t double down on loss-makers

How the system works

Connect Shopify → pull orders and line items → map revenue and discounts per line item → apply fee models (Shopify fees, payment fees) and cost inputs (COGS, packaging, shipping) → allocate refunds and ads (phase-2) → compute net profit and margin % → flag risk and generate decision prompts → log decisions and track outcomes (repricing, pause ads, update COGS).
